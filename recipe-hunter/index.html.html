Recipe~

Calabasa with Rice

Ingredients

1 lb GROUND MEAT (any).

2 or more ZUCCHINI (any color or both).

1 TOMATO SAUCE short can (or ½ of a large can, also may use 1 to 2 tablespoons tomato PASTE but add more salt).

¼ teaspoon SALT (taste and add?).

OTHER SPICES to your taste.

WATER 3 tomato sauce cans full OR enough to cover all ingredients by ¼ inch over.

RICE 1 to 2 cups

Directions

Brown MEAT in a large pan.

Slice ZUCCHINI and add to MEAT.

Add SALT and any other spices. Stir and cook for 3 minutes.

Add TOMATO SAUCE and warm-to-hot WATER right away. Stir for 1 minute.

Bring it to a BIG BOIL for 2 minutes then REDUCE HEAT and let simmer for 25 to 30 mins, checking to make sure water does not evaporate; add more if necessary.

Cook 1 to 2 cups of RICE:

1 cup of rice to 2 cups of water in a pot, bring to a BOIL, cover, then lower HEAT very very low and let cook for 10 minutes. Fluff with a fork when done.

Serve CALABASA over RICE.
