Cheesy Chicken Muffin Pizzas

Ingredients

One 4oz chicken breast-cut into cubes

1 cup (4oz) whole milk mozzarella-coarsely grated

2/3 cup baby spinach-chopped

1/2 cup (4oz) mascarpone

1/3 cup (1oz) parmesan-finely grated

1/3 cup tomato-, basil sauce-can be jar

2 TBSP fresh basil-chopped

2 tsp fresh lemon juice-about 1/2 a small lemon

1/4 tsp salt

1/4 tsp ground pepper

Nonstick vegetable oil spray

All-purpose flour for dusting

1 pound ball fresh pizza dough

Directions

1. In a large bowl combine the chicken, mozzarella, mascarpone, parmesan, tomato-basil sauce, basil, lemon juice, salt and pepper.

2. Position oven rack in the center of the oven and preheat to 425 degrees F. Spray a standard 12 cup muffin pan with nonstick spray.

3. Lightly flour a work surface and roll the pizza dough to a thin large round, about 1/8 inch thick. Using a 3 Â½ to 4 inch round cutter, cut out 12 rounds for pizza crusts. Working with 1 crust at a time, settle 1 dough round into the prepared muffin pan. Press and pull the dough up the sides of the cup. Pack in 2 to 3 TBSP of filling.

4. Bake the pizzas until the crusts are golden brown and the filling is bubbling, 16-18 minutes. Let stand 1 minute. Then use a small knife to loosen the pizzas. Arrange on a platter and serve.
